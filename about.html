About Alessio Laiso Type

Alessio Laiso Type is an independent type foundry established by Alessio Laiso in 2013. Based in Lisbon, Portugal, the foundry publishes original retail typefaces and provides custom type services for clients of all sizes around the world.

In addition to this work, Alessio Laiso speaks at design conferences and meetups, hosts workshops, and collaborates with open source projects such as Google Fonts.

A variety of international brands have used fonts from Alessio Laiso Type, including eBay, Amazon, USA Today, GlobalGiving, 7-Eleven and the Italian Space Agency.
