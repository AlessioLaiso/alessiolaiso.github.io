Custom fonts

In addition to our retail fonts, we have worked with a variety of large and small brands to help them with custom type work. Such projects include:

  • Development of new fonts from scratch.
  • Customisation or expansions of the fonts in our library (or occasionally of third-party open source fonts, when the license allows for that).
  • Custom lettering for applications such as branding.
  • Design advice for multiple typographic needs.

Interested? Feel free to get in touch at hello@alessiolaiso.com
